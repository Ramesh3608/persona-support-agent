SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA) — UPTIME & INCIDENT POLICY
Platform Support Documentation
Effective Date: January 2025

==============================================
SECTION 1: UPTIME COMMITMENTS
==============================================

Pro Plan:
- Guaranteed uptime: 99.9%
- Allowable downtime per month: 43.8 minutes
- Allowable downtime per year: 8.76 hours

Enterprise Plan:
- Guaranteed uptime: 99.99%
- Allowable downtime per month: 4.38 minutes
- Allowable downtime per year: 52.6 minutes

Uptime is calculated as: ((Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes) x 100

Scheduled maintenance windows (communicated 72 hours in advance) are excluded from uptime calculations.

==============================================
SECTION 2: INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES
==============================================

Severity 1 (Critical — Full Service Outage):
- Initial Response: Within 15 minutes
- Status Update Frequency: Every 30 minutes
- Target Resolution Time: 2 hours
- Applies to: Pro and Enterprise plans

Severity 2 (High — Major Feature Unavailable):
- Initial Response: Within 1 hour
- Status Update Frequency: Every 2 hours
- Target Resolution Time: 8 hours

Severity 3 (Medium — Partial Degradation):
- Initial Response: Within 4 hours
- Status Update Frequency: Every 24 hours
- Target Resolution Time: 3 business days

Severity 4 (Low — Minor Issues / Cosmetic Bugs):
- Initial Response: Within 24 hours
- Target Resolution Time: 2 weeks

==============================================
SECTION 3: SERVICE CREDITS
==============================================

If monthly uptime falls below the SLA guarantee, customers are entitled to:

Pro Plan Credits:
- 99.5% – 99.9%: 10% of monthly fee as service credit
- 99.0% – 99.5%: 25% of monthly fee as service credit
- Below 99.0%: 50% of monthly fee as service credit

Enterprise Plan Credits:
- 99.9% – 99.99%: 10% of monthly fee as service credit
- 99.0% – 99.9%: 25% of monthly fee as service credit
- Below 99.0%: 50% of monthly fee as service credit

Credits are applied to the next billing cycle automatically. Credits do not carry cash value.

To claim a service credit:
1. Submit a request within 30 days of the incident
2. Navigate to Billing → Service Credits → Request Credit
3. Reference the incident ticket number

==============================================
SECTION 4: PLANNED MAINTENANCE
==============================================

Standard Maintenance Window:
- Every Tuesday, 02:00–04:00 UTC
- Customers notified via email and status page 72 hours in advance

Emergency Maintenance:
- Notification sent as early as possible
- Typically limited to critical security patches
- Duration usually under 30 minutes

Monitor live system status at: https://status.platform.com

==============================================
SECTION 5: INCIDENT COMMUNICATION
==============================================

Incident updates are communicated through:
1. Status page: https://status.platform.com
2. Email notifications (ensure incidents@platform.com is not spam-filtered)
3. In-app notification banner
4. Slack integration (Enterprise customers with Slack workspace connected)

Post-Incident Report (PIR):
- Published within 5 business days of Severity 1 or 2 incidents
- Includes root cause analysis, timeline, and corrective actions
- Accessible via status page → Past Incidents

==============================================
SECTION 6: EXCLUSIONS FROM SLA
==============================================

The SLA does not apply to downtime caused by:
- Customer's own infrastructure or network
- Third-party services outside our control
- Force majeure events
- Scheduled maintenance (communicated in advance)
- Customer actions that violate terms of service
- Free plan users (no SLA guarantee)
