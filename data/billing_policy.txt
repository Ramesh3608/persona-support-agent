BILLING POLICY DOCUMENT
Platform Support — Billing & Payments
Last Updated: January 2025

==============================================
SECTION 1: SUBSCRIPTION PLANS & PRICING
==============================================

Free Plan:
- $0/month
- 100 API calls/minute
- 5GB storage
- Community support only
- No SLA guarantee

Pro Plan:
- $49/month (billed monthly) or $39/month (billed annually)
- 1,000 API calls/minute
- 100GB storage
- Email support with 24-hour response SLA
- 99.9% uptime SLA

Enterprise Plan:
- Custom pricing (contact sales@platform.com)
- Unlimited API calls
- Custom storage quotas
- Dedicated account manager
- 4-hour response SLA
- 99.99% uptime SLA

==============================================
SECTION 2: BILLING CYCLE & INVOICING
==============================================

- Billing cycles run from the 1st to the last day of each calendar month.
- Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month for the previous month.
- Invoices are sent to the primary billing email on file.
- Payment is due within 14 days of invoice generation.
- Accepted payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, SEPA Direct Debit, Bank Transfer (Enterprise only).

==============================================
SECTION 3: REFUND POLICY
==============================================

Annual Plan Refunds:
- Full refund available within 30 days of annual plan purchase if you have used fewer than 1,000 API calls.
- Prorated refund available within 90 days for unused months.
- No refund after 90 days of annual plan purchase.

Monthly Plan Refunds:
- No refunds for monthly subscriptions once the billing cycle has started.
- Exception: If service downtime exceeded our SLA in the billing period, a service credit equivalent to the downtime cost will be applied.

Duplicate Charge Refunds:
- Duplicate charges are identified and refunded within 5-7 business days.
- To report a duplicate charge, submit a ticket via the billing portal with your invoice numbers.

==============================================
SECTION 4: PAYMENT FAILURES & DUNNING
==============================================

If a payment fails:
- Day 1: Payment retry and email notification sent
- Day 3: Second payment retry
- Day 7: Third retry; account flagged as past-due
- Day 14: Service suspension; data retained for 30 days
- Day 44: Account and data permanently deleted

To prevent service interruption, ensure your payment method is current in Billing Settings.

==============================================
SECTION 5: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
==============================================

To raise a billing dispute:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Billing → Dispute a Charge
3. Select the invoice and describe the discrepancy
4. Our billing team will respond within 3 business days

All disputes must be raised within 60 days of the charge date. Disputes older than 60 days cannot be processed.

For urgent billing issues, email: billing@platform.com

==============================================
SECTION 6: TAX & COMPLIANCE
==============================================

- EU customers: VAT applied at the applicable local rate
- US customers: Sales tax applied based on state regulations
- Tax exemption: Upload your tax exemption certificate in Billing Settings
- W-9 / W-8 forms available on request via billing@platform.com
