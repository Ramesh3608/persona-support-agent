# Account Management Guide

## Creating and Managing Your Account

### Account Registration
To create a new account, visit https://platform.com/signup and provide:
- Valid business email address
- Full name and company name
- Password (minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, number, and symbol)

Email verification is required within 24 hours of registration.

---

## Password Reset

### Self-Service Password Reset
If you have forgotten your password:
1. Go to https://platform.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 15 minutes)
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Confirm the new password and click "Reset Password"

**Note:** If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. The sender address is noreply@platform.com.

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be the same as your last 5 passwords

---

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

### Enabling 2FA
1. Go to Account Settings → Security
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Choose your 2FA method: Authenticator App (recommended) or SMS
4. Follow the on-screen setup instructions
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### Supported Authenticator Apps
- Google Authenticator
- Microsoft Authenticator
- Authy

### 2FA Recovery
If you lose access to your 2FA device:
1. Use one of the 8 backup codes provided during setup
2. If backup codes are also lost, contact support@platform.com with government-issued ID for verification

---

## Account Modification Requests

The following changes require identity verification and must be submitted through the support portal:
- Email address change
- Primary billing contact change
- Account ownership transfer
- Adding or removing organization members with admin privileges

Processing time: 1-3 business days.

---

## Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account:
1. Go to Account Settings → Danger Zone
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm by typing your email address
4. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

**Warning:** Account deletion is irreversible. Export your data before proceeding via Settings → Data Export.

---

## Session Management

- Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity
- Maximum 5 concurrent sessions per account
- To view active sessions: Account Settings → Active Sessions
- You can revoke any session remotely from this panel

---

## Browser Compatibility & Cache Issues

If the platform is not loading correctly:
1. Clear your browser cache: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
2. Disable browser extensions temporarily
3. Try an incognito/private browsing window
4. Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version

Supported browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
